Any Question asked about any topic, the answer should be in the form of a paragraph. The paragraph should contain the following information:
Who? – Who is involved? (people, organizations, entities)
What? – What happened or is happening? (event, action, subject)
Where? – Where did it happen or where is it happening? (location)
When? – When did it happen or when will it happen? (time, date, timeline)
Why? – Why did it happen? (reason, cause, motivation)
Which? – Which options or choices were available? (specific decisions, alternatives)
How? – How did it happen or how will it happen? (method, process, means)
If answer is not matching response can be based on the question asked.
